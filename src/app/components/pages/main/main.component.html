Who we are?

The Consultation Center of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Albany is a mental health center which provides therapeutic services within a Christian environment, respecting the unity of body, mind and spirit. The Center offers individual and couples counseling, a variety of personal growth groups, spiritual direction, and lectures and workshops in the area of mental health and personal growth.
